Description:	This patient is displaying a bodily posture known as "opisthotonos" due to Clostridium tetani exotoxin.

Generalized tetanus, the most common type (about 80%) usually presents with a descending pattern, starting with trismus or lockjaw, followed by stiffness of the neck, difficulty in swallowing, and rigidity of abdominal muscles.
