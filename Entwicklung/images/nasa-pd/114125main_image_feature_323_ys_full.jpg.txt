Orbital 'Thumbs Up'

The Expedition 10 and 11 crews give the "thumbs up" after the change of command ceremony on the International Space Station on April 22, 2005. From left: Expedition 11 Flight Engineer John L. Phillips, Expedition 11 Commander Sergei K. Krikalev, Expedition 10 Commander Leroy Chiao, European Space Agency (ESA) astronaut Roberto Vittori of Italy; and Expedition 10 Flight Engineer Salizhan S. Sharipov.
