[edit] Description

ADR danger sign (ADR = Accord Europeen Relatif au Transport International des Marchandises Dangereuses par Route)
Dansk: Skilt for farligt gods ved transport i henhold til ADR-bestemmelserne om transport i EU. Det øverste tal angiver faregruppen. Tallet skal være på minimum to cifre, derfor tilføjes et nul her. Nederste tal angiver UN-nummeret for stoffet. Dette skilt omhandler trichlorethylen
English: Sign for hazmat (hazardous material) transport on EU roads. Top number shows danger code. Bottom number shows the UN number in the hazmat list. Top must have at least two digits, thats why a 0 is added. I don't know what Trichlorethylen is called in english?
